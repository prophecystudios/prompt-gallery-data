📖 How to Use

✨

These prompts are specially crafted for Gemini Nano Banana (yes, that's actually its name – Google wasn't messing around! 🍌)

📋

How to use: Tap the copy button, hop over to the Gemini app, paste your prompt, and watch the magic happen!

❤️

Found a prompt you love? Hit that heart button and save it to your favorites for later creative sessions!

🎲

Pro tip: Results may vary – Nano Banana has its own artistic mood swings. That's what makes it fun!

🙏

If this app sparks joy (or at least saves you from prompt writer's block), a quick ⭐ rating on Play Store would make my day! And if you're feeling extra generous, there's a "Support the Dev" button waiting for you in Settings 😉

🔒 Privacy Policy

🙅

No data collection. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is safe with us!

💾

Local storage only. Your favorites are saved locally on your device – they never leave your phone.

🎨

AI-generated images. All images in this app were created using Gemini Nano Banana. No real people, no copyrighted content.

📊

No analytics or tracking. We don't use any third-party analytics. No cookies, no trackers, no creepy stuff.

📧

Questions? Reach out via the "Send Feedback" option in Settings. We're happy to help!
